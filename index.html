5 ORCHARD DRIVE

Warehouse - Hudson Valley Area Gardiner, NY 12525

Description

For lease, 29, 545 sq. ft. of warehouse on 7 acre site. Located along NYS RT-32, 1.1 miles south of RT- 44/55, 10.3 miles / 15 minutes north of I-84 E/W and RT-17K near I-87 N/S. The ceiling height ranges from 12’-25’. The building has eight (8) loading docks, one drive-in door up a ramp. Water and sewer are well and septic. The original building was built in the 1950’s and the adjacent building add-on was built in 1985. Both are fully insulated warehouses. Possession is immediate. Currently used for ware- house with heating and air conditioning and two large storage yards. Property includes an adjacent modular office building of about 1,500 sq. ft. 800 amp, 3 phase electric, propane gas heat. Zoned BD 60 Light Business. Base rental is $9.50 per sq. ft. net.
